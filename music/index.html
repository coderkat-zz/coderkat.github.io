Hi! At times, I'm a musician!

A trumpet player, vocalist, and fledgling guitar player, I've been making music on and off about as long as I can remember. I lean towards trad jazz and folk-inspired music, but have played in symphonies and soul bands. I love it all.

While in San Francisco, I had the pleasure to work with some swell pals on a little band formerly known as the Hickory Street Jazz Club, and I'm thrilled to find friends to play and sing with as I spend this year as a vagabond.

You can check out some stuff I've worked on on Soundcloud!
