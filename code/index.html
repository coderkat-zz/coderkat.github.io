Code Monkey

In the winter of 2013, I started to learn to code and it has changed my life in so many ways. I loved it so much I enrolled in Hackbright Academy the spring of that year, and after a very intense 12 weeks, entered the workforce as a software developer.

I've since had the pleasure to work as a software engineer at Eventbrite for two years, followed by a year at the now-extinct Lovely as a developer and engineering manager. I am so grateful for the mentorship and growth I found at both of these companies.

I'm now in the midst of a year of being (more or less) a vagabond: a few months in Portland led to a summer in my hometown of Chicago, and I'vehave the pleasure to spend this fall living in Southeast Asia. I have a few projects brewing, and am excited to share them in this space when they're ready!

Oh yeah, I'm also on LinkedIn.
